# Employment Simulator

A simulation of employment — and, more broadly, of *exchanges* between agents:
labor for wages, goods for money, favors, obligations, and other transfers of
value. The aim is to model how these exchanges arise, what terms they settle on,
and how they aggregate into larger patterns over time.

## Status

Early scaffolding. The conceptual models are being worked out in
[`DEVPLAN.md`](./DEVPLAN.md); progress is logged in [`DEVLOG.md`](./DEVLOG.md).
